ABOUT LUCKY SHRUBS

Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design company that focuses on landscaping, garden construction, and upkeep. Lucky Shrub is a small plant nursery with headquarters in Taos, New Mexico. They provide clients with both indoor and outdoor plants, enabling them to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of" in one convenient location.

Jason and Maria, a husband and wife duo who have always loved plants, founded Lucky Shrub. The designer of the garden is Jason. He manages his staff of landscapers in addition to designing and supervising every design. Maria is in charge of the nursery and all marketing for the business.
